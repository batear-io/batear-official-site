Why Batear?

Edge FFT Processing

Real-time spectral analysis runs entirely on the ESP32-S3. No cloud round-trips, no latency — detection in milliseconds.

Encrypted LoRa Alerts

AES-128-GCM encrypted packets over LoRa sub-GHz. Designed for contested RF environments with anti-jamming resilience.

Home Assistant Ready

MQTT auto-discovery means plug-and-play integration. Trigger automations, log events, and monitor your airspace from your dashboard.

Hardware Flexibility

Supports Heltec WiFi LoRa 32 V3 out of the box, with a board abstraction layer ready for LILYGO PoE and other ESP32-S3 variants.

Active Field Testing

Proven Under Pressure

Batear is being field-tested in collaboration with Ukrainian defense experts in active electronic warfare environments. Our partners' identities remain confidential for their safety.
